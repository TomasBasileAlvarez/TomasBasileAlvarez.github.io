Calculus 4



Syllabus

  1. Multiple Integrals

    • Integral of a function of two variables. Riemann sums
    • Properties of integrals
    • Sets with measure zero
    • Fubini's theorem
    • Calculation of multiple integrals over rectangular domains
    • Change of variable theorem and integration in polar, cylindrical and spherical coordinates
    • Improper integrals
    • Integrals of non continous functions
    • Uniform convergence

  2. Line Integrals

    • Integration of scalar functions in parametric curves
    • Path integrals of vector functions. Work
    • Conservative fields
    • Green's theorem. Applications and examples

  3. Surface Integrals

    • Parametric surfaces. Normal vector and tangent plane
    • Integration over parametric surfaces
    • Integration of scalar and vector functions over surfaces
    • Integrals in curvilinear coordinates

  4. Theorems

    • Divergence theorem
    • Line integrals
    • Green's theorem
    • Stoke's theorem
    • Gauss' theorem
    • Flux across a surface
    • Green's identities
    • Laplacian in different coordinate systems
    • Green's function

  5. Uniform Convergence and power series

    • Definition and examples of uniform convergence
    • Weierstrass' M test
    • Examples of continuous functions no where differentiable
    • Power series
    • Taylor series and convergence intervals
    • Integrating and differentiating series

  6. Differential Forms

    • Exterior derivative. Closed and exact forms.
    • Integrals over differential forms
    • Manifolds
    • Stoke's theorem in manifolds

Bibliography:

  • Courant, R., Differential and Integral Calculus, Volumen II. New York: J. Wiley, 1936
  • Courant, R., John, F., Introducción al Cálculo y al Análisis Matemático, Volumen II. México: Limusa, 1974.
  • Apostol, T.M., Calculus, Volumen I. México: Ed. Reverté, 2001.
  • Spivak, M., Cálculo en Variedades. México: Ed. Reverté, 1987.
