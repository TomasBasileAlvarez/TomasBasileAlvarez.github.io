Algebra



Syllabus

  1. Sets and Functions

    • Sets and substes
    • Operations on sets and properties
    • Relations and functions
    • Properties of functions and operations between functions
    • Induction principle

  2. Matrices and Determinants

    • Definition and operations
    • Equivalent matrices
    • Row echelon form and elementary matrices
    • Determinant

  3. Systems of Equations

    • Solving a system of equations
    • Homogeneous and non homogeneous systems

  4. Complex Numbers

    • Field of complex numbers
    • Operations and properties
    • Polar form
    • Moivre theorem and roots of complex numbers

  5. Polynomials

    • Definitions and properties
    • Roots of polynomials
    • Factor and residue theorem

Bibliography:

  • Albert, A.A., 1967, Algebra superior , UTEHA, México.
  • Cardenas, H. Lluis E., 1990, Algebra superior , Trillas, México.
  • Birkhoff, G., MacLane, S., 1977, A survey of modern algebra , 4th edition, MacMillan, New York, USA.
