Linear Algebra 2



Syllabus

  1. Bilinear Forms and Operators

    • Bilinear forms
    • Quadratic forms
    • Adjoint operators
    • Unitary, hermitian and orthogonal operators
    • Sylvester theorem

  2. Diagonalization

    • Eigen vectors and eigen values
    • Characteristical polynomial
    • Diagonalization and basis of proper vectors

  3. Triangulation

    • Existance of a triangulation over the complex field
    • Hamilton Cayley theorem
    • Diagonalization of unitary operators

  4. Spectral Theorem

    • Symmetrical operators over the real field
    • Symmetrical operators over the complex field

  5. Jordan Canonical Form

    • Primary decomposition
    • Jordan canonical form
