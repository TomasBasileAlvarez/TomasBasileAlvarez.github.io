Tensor Calculus



Syllabus

  1. Introduction

    • Vector Spaces. Homomorphisms and linear transformations
    • Topology. Basic concepts and topological spaces
    • Vectors. Inner product and representations in coordinate systems
    • Indical notation. Einstein notation
    • Covariant and contravariant vectors

  2. Tensor Algebra

    • Operations of tensor
    • Tensor product
    • Properties of tensors: Representation, transpose, symmetry
    • Identity tensor, Levi Civita tensor
    • Transformation of components of a tensor

  3. Differential Geometry

    • Curves
    • Surfaces
    • Manifolds
    • Tangent space
    • One forms and cotangent space
    • Formal definition of a tensor
    • Connections
    • Covariant derivative
    • Fundamental theorem of Riemannian geometry
    • Geodesics and parallel transport

  4. Field Theory

    • Symmetries
    • Noether's theorem
    • Electromagnetism in tensor notation
    • General theory of relativity

Bibliography:

  • Boothby, W. M. (2010). An introduction to differentiable manifolds and Riemannian geometry. Amsterdam: Academic Press.
  • Kobayashi, S., & Nomizu, K. (1996). Foundations of differential geometry. New York, NY: Wiley.
  • Schutz, B. F. (1999). Geometrical methods of mathematical physics. Cambridge: Cambridge University Press.
