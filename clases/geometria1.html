Analytical Geometry 1



Syllabus

  1. Introduction

    • Elementary geometry concepts (distances, lines, points)
    • Cartesian coordinates
    • Geometrical objects defined by equations

  2. Trigonometry

    • Trigonometric relations
    • Triangles
    • Trigonometric functions and identities
    • Polar coordinates
    • Spherical and cylindrical coordinates

  3. Vector Spaces

    • Definition and examples
    • Subspaces
    • Linear independence, base and dimension
    • Scalar and vector product

  4. Lines and Planes

    • Equations for a line in 2 and 3 dimensions
    • Parametric and cartesian equations of a plane
    • Systems of linear equations

  5. Conics

    • Definitions and equations
    • Translation of conics
    • Rotations in \( R^2 \)
    • Parametrized conics

Bibliography:

  • Bracho, J. Geometría Analítica. Notas.
  • Efimov, N. Geometría Superior. Moscú: MIR., 1984
  • Preston, G. C., Lovaglia, A. R., Modern Analytic Geometry. New York: Harper & Row, 1971.
