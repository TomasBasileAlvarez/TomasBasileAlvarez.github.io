Contemporary Physics



Syllabus

  1. Classical Mechanics

    • Kinematics: Speed, acceleration, free fall, parabolic motion
    • Dynamics: Force, free body diagrams, moment, energy
    • Gravitational Force: Kepler laws, law of universal gravitation

  2. Electromagnetism

    • Electric charges and Coulumb's law
    • Electric field and electric potential
    • Electrical current, Magnetic fields and forces.

  3. Thermodynamics

    • Energy and equilibrium states
    • Ideal gases
    • Work and heat, first law of thermodynamics
    • Second law of thermodynamics

  4. Quantum Mechanics

    • Atomic models
    • Atomic spectrum
    • Black body radiation and photoelectric effect

Bibliography:

  • Halliday, Resnick, Walker. Fundamentos de Física. Vol. I , 8ª. Edición, Grupo Editorial Patria (2011).
  • Halliday, Resnick, Walker. Fundamentos de Física. Vol. II , 8ª. Edición, Grupo Editorial Patria (2011).
