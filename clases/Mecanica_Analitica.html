Analytical Mechanics



Syllabus

  1. Dynamics of a particle

    • Newton's laws
    • Forces
    • Potential and kinetic energy
    • Movement in one dimension. Harmonic oscillator, resonance, Duffing oscillator
    • Movement in two and three dimensions. Projectiles with friction, pendulum, perturbation theory

  2. Central Fields

    • Conservation theorems
    • Kepler's problem
    • Elliptical orbits
    • Satellites

  3. Systems of particles

    • Center of mass
    • Two body problem
    • Collisions and dispersion
    • Three body problem

  4. Lagrangian and Hamiltonian Mechanics

    • Generalized coordinates
    • Euler Lagrange equation
    • Principles of conservation
    • Hamiltonian equations
    • Examples

  5. Systems of reference

    • Accelerated systems
    • Coriolis force

  6. Rigid body

    • Rotation of a rigid body
    • Moment of inertia and Euler's angles
    • Symmetric top. Precession and nutation

  7. Small Vibrations

    • Oscillators
    • Normal modes

Bibliography:

  • Barger, V.D., Olsson, M.G., 1995, Classical mechanics: A modern perspective , McGraw­Hill, N.Y., USA
  • Marion, J.B., 1992, Dinámica clásica de las partículas y sistemas , Editorial Reverté, Barcelona, España.
  • Landau, L.D., Lifshitz, E.M., 1986, Mecánica , Ed. Reverté, Barcelona, España.
