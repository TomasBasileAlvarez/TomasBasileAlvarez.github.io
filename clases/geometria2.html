Analytical Geometry 2



Syllabus

  1. Cuadric Surfaces

    • Cylinders
    • Surfaces of revolution generated by conics
    • The second order equation without mixed terms
    • Symmetries
    • Cuadric surfaces with axis parallel to coordinate axes
    • Tangent plane

  2. Transformations

    • Linear Transformations
    • Matrix of a linear transformation
    • Rigid transformations
    • Elimination of mixed terms in the general second order equation in three dimensions using a rotation.
    • Affine transformations

  3. Geometry of the Sphere

    • Spherical coordinates
    • Spherical trigonometry
    • Geodesics

  4. Mobius Transformations

    • Geometrical interpretation of sum and product of complex numbers
    • Extended complex plane
    • Mobius transformations and properties.

Bibliography:

  • Bracho, J. Geometría Analítica. Notas.
  • Efimov, N. Geometría Superior. Moscú: MIR., 1984
  • Preston, G. C., Lovaglia, A. R., Modern Analytic Geometry. New York: Harper & Row, 1971.
