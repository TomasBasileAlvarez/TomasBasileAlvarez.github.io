Now we divide the data into train and test data. To do it, we define a function that takes the first $p$ percent of entries into the train data and the rest into test data.

In [14]:
def train_test(data,p):
    train = data[:int(len(data)*p)]
    test = data[int(len(data)*p):]
    return(train,test)

#Lets do it for p = 0.8 for the data of the original time series (not the returns, we will do that later)
